Hello, This is Luis Espejo

Hello, This is Luis Espejo. Luis and I have been friends for about two years.

Luis Espejo and I have been friends for the past two years. He is a Venezuelan native. Because of the civil instability in Venezuela, Luis relocated to the United States. Just like most Immigrates coming to the United States from non-English speaking countries, He has had his own challenges with English and, of course, challenges of transitioning. Regardless of all those challenges, Luis triumphs. Luis now works and speaks English fluently.

Luis is quite a busy man, he works six days a week. However, he managed to do so much in the little time he gets. Fun fact, Luis and I met at a soccer field after his team lost to my team. Luis plays goalkeeper for a local second-division team. Luis is also the captain and goalkeeper for Team Venezuela in the International Unity Cup league hosted every summer in the city to bring people together.
